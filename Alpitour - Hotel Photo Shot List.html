Alpitour B2B & B2C · image production

Hotel & resort photo shot list

Every <image-slot> across the Alpitour prototypes that needs a genAI-generated photographic image. Slots are grouped by subject — generate one image per hotel/resort/destination and reuse it across the listed slot IDs. The prompts are written to hand straight to an image-gen tool.

How to use this

  1. Generate one photo per subject below using its prompt. Render at least at the largest crop listed (most are landscape ~16:9; portraits/squares are flagged).
  2. Open the matching prototype, then drag the image onto each slot with the listed id. Dropped images persist in a single project-level .image-slots.state.json, so they survive reloads and show on share links.
  3. Slot IDs are global. Two slots with the same id (e.g. hotel-hero-coral across the proposal and the simple front door) share one image — fill it once. Different IDs of the same hotel (e.g. hotel-coral vs hotel-hero-coral) are separate slots but can reuse the same source photo at different crops.
  4. Most resorts are fictional brand names — there's no real property to match. Generate a plausible, on-type resort photo; no signage, logos, or readable text in frame.
